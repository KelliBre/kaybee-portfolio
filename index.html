About Me

I am from a small town in Georgia, but I have bounced between Guam and San Diego for most of my adult life and happily claim both as part of what makes me…well…me.

My approach to web development is simple. I like clean, semantic and accessible code that is ready for collaboration and growth.

When I am not at the keyboard you can find me somewhere near or in the water. Sometimes it’s in a set of fins and a mask free diving. Sometimes it’s as a volunteer with the Surfrider Foundation, a fabulous group of people dedicated to protecting our oceans and our access to it.
